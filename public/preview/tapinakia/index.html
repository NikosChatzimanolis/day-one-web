Our Story

A Table Made
for Sharing

At Ta Pinakia, dining is built around the Greek tradition of sharing. From classic meze dishes and grilled favourites to carefully selected drinks, every visit is designed to feel generous, familiar, and full of character.

Built around flavour, warmth, and the joy of sharing, we offer a menu inspired by classic meze culture and comforting dishes — evenings that bring people together at the centre of Paphos.
